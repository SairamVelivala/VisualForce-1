Linvio Non-Profit Toolkit

We know you need to be able to pick and choose technologies to run your non-profit operation, but have a hard time finding components that are interoperable and flexible enough to meet your needs. So our non-profit toolkit includes support for donation management, memberships, online ticket sales, simple webstore landing pages, and more – you can select the components that make sense for your organization. They all work together as add-ons to PaymentConnect in the Force.com environment!

Key Features

  • Click-to-Configure Donation Landing Pages
  • Membership Fees
  • Contact Management
  • Fundraising Events
Request a Demo

Click-to-Configure Donation Landing Pages

Using the Linvio Donations Expansion Pack, you can easily configure and publish multiple types of donation landing pages hosted in your Salesforce instance. Just create a new donation page record, set the options for donation amount, header HTML and other page configuration settings and save to publish it to your Salesforce Sites website.

Your donation landing pages can be styled with CSS and Salesforce Site template tools to make each page compatible with your online image, and the donation forms enhanced to collect custom field data with just a few clicks. New donor information is inserted or updated in Salesforce in real-time along with donation amounts, history, and your custom field values.

Membership Fees

Collect recurring membership fees with Linvio eStore Lite. In addition to supporting the sale of individual items on your Salesforce Sites website, this add-on includes click-to-configure subscription pages that are perfect for setting up your members with a recurring payment plan.

Learn More

Contact Management

Whether your organization uses the standard Salesforce Contact/Account model for storing donor information, or Person Accounts, PaymentConnect and the Donations expansion pack will work in your instance of Salesforce. If you use Person Accounts, we offer a free batch utility that will convert contact information related to donations into Person Account records at regularly scheduled intervals throughout the day.

 

Fundraising Events

Plan, promote, publish, and manage your fundraising events with our Premier Native Event Management add-on for Salesforce, Linvio Events. This package includes support for multiple types of events, allowing you to publish event details to a Salesforce Sites event micro-site, collect online registration fees and donations, and track registration/attendance details in Salesforce.
